Sponge Cake

Rating

★★★★★

Portions

ca. 10 balls

Ingredients

50 g Raisins
50 g Dates
25 g Walnuts
25 g Figs
25 g Apricots (dried)
25 g Almonds
25 g Cashew
25 g Oatmeal

Steps

  1. Shred/Chop/Mix all ingredients with a powerful mixer
  2. Form little balls

Additonal Notes

Roll the sticky energy balls in cocos flakes, cacao, sesam, poppy, ... This makes them even tastier and nicer to grip.
