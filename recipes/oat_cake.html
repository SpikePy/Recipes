Oat Cake

Rating

★★★★☆

Portions

1Loaf Pan

Ingredients

250 g Oatmeal
250 ml (Soy/Oat) Milk
40 g Butter/Coconut Oil (liquid)
1-2 Piece Egg/Banana
1-2 Spoon Maple Syrup
½ Tee Spoon Baking Soda
1 Pinch Salt
1 Knive Tip Cinnamon
250 g Fruits (Blueberry, Apple, Banana, ...)

Steps

  1. Mix all ingedients except the cholocalte in a Loaf Pan
  2. Put it in the oven for 30 minutes at 180℃

Additonal Notes

  • Feeld free to add additional stuff to the cake e.g. chocolate pieces or nuts.
  • If you like you could spread 100 g of any chocolate you like on top of the cakes surface 5min before you should get it out of the oven. Distribute it evenly when it is stil warm and fluid. After the cake cooled down, put it in the freezer to make the chocolate solid again.
