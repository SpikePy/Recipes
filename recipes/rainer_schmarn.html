Rainer-Schmarn ★★★★☆

Portions

4

Ingredients

1 unit Oatmeal
1 unit Yoghurt/Quark
Milk
1 pc Egg
1 Tablespoon Sugar
1 pc Fruit (Banana/Blueberry/Raisins/...)[OPTIONAL]
1 Knife Tip Cinnamon [OPTIONAL]

Steps

The specified quantities are just guidelines. Change them to your tasting.

  1. If you decided to add fruits slice them in tiny pieces.
  2. Mix everything in a bowl.
  3. Fry it in a pan.
  4. If you are lucky it stays in one piece if you try to turn it around in the pan. Else divide it into smaller pieces and fry it like Kaiserschmarn.

Additonal Notes

  • Eat as is or put a bit of marmalade/acorn syrup/honey/sugar/cinnamon or whatever you like on top if you like to.
